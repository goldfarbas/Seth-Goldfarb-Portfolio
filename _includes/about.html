About

Seth Goldfarb is a Content Marketing professional who helps emerging tech companies tell the stories of their success. He's spent the past two years providing freelance marketing and content management services to blockchain startups and his experience encompasses the production of written, audio (podcast), and video content as well as events and event-related content.

As an active community organizer, Seth hosts several monthly blockchain meetups in the Seattle area and maintains a calendar of blockchain events in the greater Seattle area at https://www.pnwblockchain.com/.
