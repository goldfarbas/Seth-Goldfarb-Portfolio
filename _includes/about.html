About

Seth Goldfarb is a Content Marketing professional who's passionate about helping emerging tech companies tell the stories of their success. His experience encompasses the production of written, audio (podcast), and video content as well as events and event-related content. He has provided freelance marketing and content production services to blockchain startups since June, 2017.

An active community organizer, Seth hosts several monthly blockchain meetups in the Seattle area and maintains a calendar of all blockchain meetups in the greater Seattle area at [www.pnwblockchain.com](https://www.pnwblockchain.com/).
